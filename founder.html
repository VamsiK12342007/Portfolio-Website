About the founder

Vamsi Kurli is the founder of KVP Pictures is a graduate from Film and Television Program from one of the most Prestigious Program in Western Canada The founder produced, directed, edited a feature film "She's Back In My Life" which was released in Edmonton Alberta, Canada and currently aired by multiple TV Networks such as Shaw TV. The feature film has been selecte by multiple film festivals such a Toronto Film Festival, Vancouver Festival, Calgary Film Festival and Edmonton Film Festival to nam a few within Canada.
